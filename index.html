About the Poet

Sumithdeepan A. is a contemporary poet who writes with restraint, honesty, and emotional precision. His poetry explores love that lingers, pain that matures, and the quiet strength hidden in everyday life. Drawing inspiration from personal experiences and unspoken stories, his words feel intimate—like a conversation you didn’t know you needed. Poetry, for him, is not meant to impress, but to connect.

Featured Work

Between Two Silences

I learned that not all endings are loud—
some leave quietly,
like breath on a mirror.

Letters I Never Sent

Every unsent word
still knew your name,
and waited for forgiveness.

The Shape of Waiting

Time didn’t move—
it sat beside me,
asking why hope still stayed.
